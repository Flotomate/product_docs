Share a ReportÂ¶

The product allows you to send a Report as an email. To export a Report:

  • Go to the Report List View.
  • Click on the name of a Report that you want to share. The Custom Reports page opens.
  • Click on Export in the top right. A dialog box opens where you have to enter an email/emails and select a format.
