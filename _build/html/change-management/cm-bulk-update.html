Bulk UpdateÂ¶

The product allows Technicians to change details of multiple Changes at once.

To perform Bulk Update:

  • Go to the List View.

  • Select more than one Changes.

  • The Bulk Update button appears above the list area. Click on Bulk Update and a dialog box opens.

You can change the following things:

  1. Change the assignee of the Changes.

  2. Modify the Technician Group.

  3. Change five classifiers ( Priority, Urgency, Impact, Risk and Change Type).

  4. Add new tags or append to the existing ones.

  5. Change Category, Source, Department and Location.

  6. In addition to the above you can also set the following:

    1. Change Implementer: It is the Technician who implements the Change.
    2. Change Manager: It is the Technician who supervises the entire Change process.
    3. Change Reviewer: It is the Technician who audits the implementation of the Change.
  • When you are done hit Update (number of Changes to be changed is shown) and your changes are saved.
