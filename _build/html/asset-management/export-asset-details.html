Exporting Asset Details¶

You can export the details of any Asset in the CMDB as a PDF file locally. There are two ways to do it.

Exporting from Asset List View:¶

  • Go to the Asset List View of any class.
  • Click on the Action Menu of the Asset that you want to export, and then select Export Asset.
  • You are asked to select where to save the file. Choose your location and save the file.

Exporting from Asset Details View:¶

  • Go to the Asset Details View of the Asset that you want to export.
  • Click on the Action Menu of the Asset and then select Export Asset.
  • You are asked to select where to save the file. Choose your location and save the file.
