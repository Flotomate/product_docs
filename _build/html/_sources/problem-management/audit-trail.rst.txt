.. _pm-audit-trail:
Audit Trail
***********

The Audit Trail dialog box lists most changes concerning assignment and
:ref:`classifiers <classify-a-problem>` of a Problem.

1. Go to **Problem** >> :doc:`Problem List View <problem-list-view>`.

2. Select the Problem whose Audit Trail you want to see. The Audit Trail
   is visible under the **Activity** tab.

.. _pmf-69:
.. figure:: https://s3-ap-southeast-1.amazonaws.com/flotomate-resources/problem-management/PM-69.png
    :align: center
    :alt: figure 69

3. Alternatively, click on the Action Menu icon next to
   **Assign**/Name-of-Assignee, and click **View Audit Trail**.

.. _pmf-70:
.. figure:: https://s3-ap-southeast-1.amazonaws.com/flotomate-resources/problem-management/PM-70.png
    :align: center
    :alt: figure 70

Another way to view Audit Trail is from the Details View:

-  Go to the :ref:`Details View <problem-details-view>` of the Problem.

-  Click on the Action Menu next to Assign/Name-of-Assignee and click on
   :ref:`View Audit Trail <pm-audit-trail>`.
